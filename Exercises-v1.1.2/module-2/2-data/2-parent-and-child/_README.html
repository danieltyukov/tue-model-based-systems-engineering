4TC00 Exercises

2. Parent and Child

A four year old wants a cookie and asks her father “Can I have a cookie?”. Her father knows that sugar isn’t good for her so tells her no. Naturally the four year old asks again, but this time adds “Please?” to her request. Same result. Each time the child asks the question she adds another “Please?”.

Eventually the father runs out of patience and gives her a cookie. The four year old happily eats the cookie, immediately followed by the question: “Can I have another cookie?”

Create a model that reflects this scenario. Model two automata, one for the father and one for his child.

Use the events no and give_cookie for the father. The father’s patience is modeled as an integer starting at 3 and decreasing by one each time the child asks and the father says no. When the father gives the cookie, his patience resets to 3.

Use the event ask for the child, and use a discrete variable of type string to store the question. The question that the father hears, is the value of the string right before the event ask.

The child is relentless and will keep asking for cookies, forever.

A template of the model is given in the ask-template.cif file.

ask-template.cif
event ask, give_cookie;

automaton Child:
    disc string question = "Can I have a cookie?";

    location:
        initial;
        edge ask              ...
        edge give_cookie      ...

end

automaton Parent:
    disc int patience = 3;

    location:
        initial;
        edge ask              ...
        edge give_cookie      ...
end
